About Us

Bramha Café is where South India’s timeless flavours meet Bengaluru’s modern café culture. 🍃 Nestled in the heart of HSR Layout, we serve authentic South Indian delicacies — from buttery Benne Dosas and soft Idlis to aromatic Filter Coffee brewed the traditional way. Every dish is prepared with love, purity, and premium ingredients, staying true to our roots while offering an elevated dining experience. With warm interiors, soulful music, and the aroma of freshly ground coffee, Bramha Café isn’t just a restaurant — it’s an experience that celebrates South Indian heritage in every bite. 🌿
